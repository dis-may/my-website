How to use

Press a button/key to save a point in the video.

Press it again to return to that point.

Overwrite a previous cue point by holding [Shift], then pressing the button/key

Use left and right arrow keys for skipping through the left video

Use [Z] and [C] for skipping through the right video

If the video does not play, it may be because YouTube does not allow some videos to be played in an embed
